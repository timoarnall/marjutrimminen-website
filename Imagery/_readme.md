# Imagery — source archive

A small archive of master assets gathered for the site, kept separate from the prototype's working images. The site's actual rendered images live in `../Design/v1/images/` and are documented there.

## Contents

- `portrait-studio.jpg` — 4096×2399, colour. Marjut at her desk in her London studio, surrounded by archive shelves and a pinboard of clippings. Photographed by Timo Arnall. Master file.
- `portrait-studio-bw.jpg` — same shot, black and white. This is the version used in the About section of the site. The site copy lives at `../Design/v1/images/portrait-studio-bw.jpg`.
- `trickyfilms_logo.gif` — the original Tricky Films logo from the 2002 trickyfilms.com site. Reference only — the new site is for marjutrimminen.com, not Tricky Films.
