# Imagery used by v1

Every image referenced from `Design/v1/index.html` lives here. Folders are per-film; portraits sit at the root.

All images are above 640px on at least one side.

## Per-film folders

### `some-protection/` (1987)
Nine 1024×768 stills sourced from Marjut's own Flickr (`flickr.com/photos/marjut/albums/72157602249631151`). Three are referenced on the site:

- `sp-prison-cell.jpg` — Josie in a prison cell. Was tagged "The Stain10" on Flickr but on inspection is *Some Protection*; reattributed.
- `sp-fairground.jpg` — Josie outside the prison, "GO away" graffiti. Originally `2773779937.jpg` (Flickr photo ID); renamed for clarity.
- `sp-hmp-gates.jpg` — Josie outside HMP gates. Was tagged "Learned by Heart 1_07" on Flickr; same misattribution, reattributed.

The remaining six (`27737799…`) are unused on the site but kept as a master set for future variations. Flickr photo IDs preserved as filenames so they can be cross-referenced with the source album.

### `the-stain/` (1991)
Two stills from Flickr's "The Stain" tag, all puppet/drawn animation from the film proper.

- `The-Stain05.jpg` — puppet figures around the dining table.
- `The-Stain07.jpg` — drawn family scene with pram and twin men in red.

### `learned-by-heart/` (2007)
Nineteen stills from Marjut's Flickr "Learned by Heart" tag. Three are on the site (`Learned-by-Heart-1_01`, `4-31`, `4-50`); the rest are an archive. The numeric segments in filenames (`1_01`, `4-31`, etc.) come from the source numbering — episode and frame.

### `many-happy-returns/` (1996)
Two 752×576 stills (`Many-Happy-Returns-02`, `Many-Happy-Returns-03`).

### `urpo-turpo/` (1996)
Four 1024×~740 stills sourced from Handle Productions (`handleproductions.com/film/urpo-turpo-en/`) — the current distributor of the series. `UT-2` and `UT-1` are used; `UT-3` and `UT-4` are kept as alternates.

### `not-feminist/` (1986)
One 800×613 still from Cinenova's archive page (`cinenova.org/artist/marjut-rimminen/`), filename `feminist-cinenova.jpg`.

## Portraits

- `portrait-studio-bw.jpg` — 4096×2399, black and white. Marjut at her studio desk surrounded by her archive shelves and pinboard. Used in the About section.
- `portrait-studio.jpg` — same shot in colour. Available if a colour treatment is ever wanted.

These are duplicated in `../../../Imagery/` (the master archive); the copies here are the actual files referenced by the page. If they're updated, replace both.

## Films without imagery on the site

Mixed Feelings, Absolut Marjut Rimminen, The Frog King, The Bridge, Trip to Eternity, plus Trailers and Idents. Marjut may have higher-resolution stills offline; otherwise possible sources are her own video / film masters (16mm, 35mm, BetaSP, Digibeta), Channel 4 archives (most short films were C4 commissions), the BFI, Jayne Pilling's *Women and Animation*, or festival catalogues (Tampere, Annecy, Hiroshima, Stuttgart Trickfilm).

## Provenance summary

All imagery on the site comes from public, attributed sources:

- Marjut's own Flickr — Some Protection, The Stain, Learned by Heart, Many Happy Returns
- Handle Productions — Urpo and Turpo (with permission implicit in their public catalogue)
- Cinenova — I'm not a Feminist, but…
- Photographer's original (Timo Arnall) — studio portrait of Marjut

Two of Marjut's Flickr images were misattributed in the source album (tagged with one film's name but actually depicting another) and have been corrected here.
